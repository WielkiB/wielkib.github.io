About Me

I am a second-degree student at the Kielce University of Technology. I earned my engineering degree in February 2024 with a grade of 5. The topic of my thesis was "Design and Implementation of a Geographical Educational Game." It was one of many games developed during my studies, both as part of projects and individual initiatives. Besides creating mechanics, I handle all necessary assets such as 3D models, animations, textures, and audio files.

My goal is to create games, putting all my knowledge, passion, and experience into them. I strive to pay attention to the smallest details because I believe that they create a certain atmosphere in games. I quickly learn new technologies that help create both games and various assets tailored to the needs of different projects. People perceive me as an extremely creative person full of crazy ideas.
